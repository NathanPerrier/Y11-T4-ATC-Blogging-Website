Exploring the World of Religion

Religion is a subject that invites students to embark on a profound journey of exploration and understanding. In this course, we delve into the rich tapestry of beliefs, traditions, and practices that have shaped cultures and societies throughout history. Students will have the opportunity to study the world's major religions, such as Christianity, Islam, Buddhism, Hinduism, and Judaism, as well as lesser-known belief systems.

Ethical Reflection and Personal Growth

Beyond the study of religious traditions, our Religion program encourages students to engage in ethical reflection and personal growth. It prompts them to grapple with questions of morality, justice, and the human condition. Students explore how religious and philosophical ideas have influenced ethical principles and continue to shape ethical dilemmas in contemporary society.
