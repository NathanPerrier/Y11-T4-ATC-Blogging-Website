Hospitality

Hospitality is a subject that merges cooking and creativity to transform raw ingredients into delicious and nutritious dishes. Students in this program explore the science behind food, learning about food safety, nutrition, and the culinary arts. They gain practical skills in food preparation, menu planning, and the use of kitchen equipment.

Digital Solutions

Digital Solutions is a subject that empowers students with the skills to navigate and innovate in our increasingly digital world. In this program, students explore topics like coding, web development, and digital design. They learn the fundamentals of programming languages such as Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, gaining the ability to create websites, apps, and interactive digital solutions.

Design and Contruction

Design and Construction is a dynamic field that combines creativity with practicality. In this subject, students delve into the fascinating world of architectural design, engineering principles, and the construction process. It's all about turning innovative ideas into tangible structures. Students learn how to create detailed architectural plans, work with construction materials, and understand the principles of sustainable design.
