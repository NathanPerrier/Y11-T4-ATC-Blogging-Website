Essential Mathematics

In our Essential Mathematics program at Ambrose Treacy College, we provide a strong foundation in fundamental mathematical concepts. This course is designed to equip students with the essential mathematical skills needed for everyday life. From budgeting to data analysis, Essential Mathematics covers it all, ensuring that our students have the tools they need to excel in practical mathematical applications.

General Mathematics

General Mathematics at Ambrose Treacy College offers students a comprehensive exploration of mathematical topics that are relevant to a wide range of fields. This course delves into areas such as statistics, algebra, and financial mathematics, preparing students for real-world problem-solving and decision-making. General Mathematics is a versatile choice for those seeking a well-rounded mathematical education.

Mathematical Methods

This course extends students' mathematical knowledge and problem-solving skills. It's an ideal choice for those pursuing careers in science, engineering, or mathematics-related fields.

Specialist Mathematics

If you're passionate about mathematics and aspire to tackle the most complex mathematical problems, Specialist Mathematics is for you. This advanced course covers topics like calculus, linear algebra, and advanced statistics, preparing students for higher-level studies in mathematics.
