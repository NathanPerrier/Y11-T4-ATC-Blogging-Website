1. Introduction

At Ambrose Treacy College ("Company", "we", "our", "us"), we believe in being transparent about how we collect and use data. This policy provides information about how and when we use cookies.

2. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a site. It enables the site to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

3. How do we use cookies?

We may use cookies to enhance the functionality of our website, to provide you with a better user experience, and to understand how our website is being used. For example, we may use cookies to:

  • Remember your login information.

  • Understand how you interact with our website.

  • Track and understand your preferences to offer you a personalized experience.

  • Conduct research and diagnostics to improve our services.

    • 4. Types of cookies

    4.1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

    4.2. Analytical/Performance Cookies

    These allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around our website when they are using it.

    4.3. Functionality Cookies

    These are used to recognize you when you return to our website and to remember choices you make to provide you with personalized content.

    4.4. Targeting Cookies

    These are used to recognize you when you return to our website and to remember choices you make to provide you with personalized content.

    5. Third-Party Cookies
