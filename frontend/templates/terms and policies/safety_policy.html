1. Introduction

This document serves as a comprehensive guideline and policy document addressing the safety concerns related to the use of AI technology by Ambrose Treacy College ("Company", "we", "our", "us"). It covers areas of potential liability and other important safety considerations regarding the Site and/or Services.

2. General AI Safety Precautions

While AI technology can be highly beneficial, it also comes with certain risks. AI misuse can lead to consequences such as data breaches, privacy invasion, and unintended actions. To mitigate these risks, Ambrose Treacy College employs rigorous AI safety precautions, including secure software design practices, and timely patches and updates.

3. Data Privacy and Protection

Ambrose Treacy College is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of user data. We comply with all applicable data protection regulations and employ robust encryption techniques to secure data. Please read our Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Content Moderation and Censorship

Ambrose Treacy College’s AI is capable of generating various types of content. While we aim to foster creativity and innovation, we are also conscious of the potential misuse of this capability. We, therefore, employ AI-driven content moderation to prevent the creation of inappropriate or harmful content.

5. Fair Usage Policy

Ambrose Treacy College’s AI technology and it's service(s) are designed to be used by humans. We do not allow the use of our services by automated bots or other non-human entities. We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate this policy.

Ambrose Treacy College encourages its users to make fair and responsible use of our services. We reserve the right to limit or restrict access to our services if a user’s usage significantly exceeds average usage patterns.

6. Accessibility and Inclusivity

We strive to make our AI services accessible and inclusive. We recognize the diversity of our user base and ensure that our AI models do not inadvertently reinforce harmful stereotypes or biases.

Furthermore, our AI models are designed to be accessible to users with disabilities. We are committed to complying with all applicable accessibility regulations and standards.

7. Third-Party Involvement

Some aspects of our services involve third-party AI providers. We carefully vet these providers to ensure they meet our strict standards for data privacy, security, and ethical AI usage.

8. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

Ambrose Treacy College complies with all applicable laws and regulations related to AI and data privacy. We ensure all our practices are up to date and in line with best current standards.
